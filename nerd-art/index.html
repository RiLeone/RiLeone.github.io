Nerd Art

Enjoy some Nerd Art. All of the images have been generated with Python scripts and the source code can be found on GitHub (some projects might still be set to private).

City Skylines

Cities are assemblies of buildings, disposed in a sort of regular grid. One could generate cities by randomly bringing together buildings. How would that look like? Here are some examples, far from being complete. These images could serve as backgorund to drawings or games.

Universe

How do you illustrate our stellar neighborhood? How do you fit the solar system into a single image? How can you generate a spiral galaxy image from code? These are some of the driving quesitons of this section.


Fractals

Fractals are fascinating: structures repeating themselves often emerging from simple, iterative processes. Here are some colored Julia sets.


Basically Circles

Many "things" can be simplified and contrained to a circle. Reducing subjects to their essentials while still clearly communicating their identity was the goal of this project.


Tracking

Have you ever jogged while tracking yourself? Have you ever wondered how your tracks add up?
